Portfolio

Track-Align Plug-in

This plugin is designed to seamlessly integrate with any Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). It aligns the timing of one or more vocal and instrument tracks, ensuring perfect synchronization. Additionally, it can match the pitch across similar tracks, enhancing the overall harmony of the mix. The project is set to be implemented in C++, leveraging its efficiency and performance.

Room Reverb Analyzer

This application analyzes the reverb characteristics of a listener's room. By using a ribbon microphone and measuring the room's height, length, and width, it assesses how sound bounces within the space. This analysis assists users in optimizing their room's acoustics for superior sound engineering and audio mixing. The project will be developed in C++, ensuring robust performance and precision.
